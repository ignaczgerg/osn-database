What is Organic Solvent Nanofiltration?

Organic Solvent Nanofiltration (OSN) or Solvent Resistant Nanofiltration (SRNF) is a pressure-driven membrane separation technology that is capable of distinguishing and separating solutes based on the physicochemical parameters. OSN offers a low-energy and continuous tool with extensive use in the pharmaceutical, food, and fine chemical industry.

OSN has been primarily used in petrochemical and pharmaceutical industries [1], solute recovery [2, 3], solvent recycling [4], and purification [5]. In addition, OSN is considered a green and potentially sustainable separation technology due to its low energy consumption and waste generation.
